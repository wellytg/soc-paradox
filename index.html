The Measurement Paradox

SOCs often spend millions on metrics that reward operational busyness rather than actual risk reduction. When analysts are evaluated on speed (MTTR) or volume, they are incentivized to optimize for the "wrong" behaviors—clearing the queue instead of securing the organization.

"The boss wants security. The analyst is rewarded for clearing the inbox."

Research Objective

To develop a Goal-Metric Mapping Framework that links analyst tasks to strategic SOC goals and makes measurement misalignment visible, explainable, and actionable.
